The Alternatives

   
De-Dupe Tools

De-dupe tools are cheap, but they only use algorithms, so fuzzy matches are often resolved incorrectly, leaving the data dirty or forcing you to manually review matches row by row.

   
Master Data Management

MDM solutions are thorough, but expensive and complex to setup and maintain.

   
Outsourced Providers

Outsourced providers may charge low hourly rates, but most don't provide audit trail or undo features, and many don't leverage the combination of algorithms and data analysts to bring down total expense.

   
In-House

In-house data cleanup uses time of your most valuable resources - your staff - and it's probably not what anyone on the team wants to be (or should be) spending their time on.
