Working at LeanData

Who said Enterprise Software isn't fun?

A Fun Work Environment

At LeanData, we work hard and play hard. You can often find us engaged in an intense game of ping pong while taking a break from coding our next big feature.

We are working hard to help our customers save millions of dollars by improving and making sense of vast amount of sales and marketing data. On a daily basis, you will find yourself working on data analytics, visualization tools, or improving our FuzzyMatching algorithm. We move fast but we also value code quality.

It's an open environment where we value everyone's contribution to the team.

Great Location

We're located in Downtown Sunnyvale, right in the heart of Silicon Valley. Sunnyvale's Historic Murphy Avenue offers many options for lunch and dinner as well as several bars to grab a drink or watch a sports game.

Our office is directly adjacent to the Sunnyvale Caltrain Station which makes for an easy commute from San Francisco or anywhere in the Bay Area.

Benefits & Perks

  • Commuter Benefits (Caltrain)
  • Health benefits
  • Vacation/PTO
  • Really smart colleagues
  • Transparent work environment and exposure to other teams
  • Ping Pong breaks everyday!
