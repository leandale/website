About Us

Company Overview

At LeanDataTM, we are focused on making our customers more successful by solving the garbage-in/garbage-out paradigm of enterprise data. We believe enterprises cannot make data-driven business decisions if they do not trust the quality of their data.

The name LeanData is derived from the core idea behind lean manufacturing, lean software development, and lean startups - maximizing customer value while minimizing waste. In order to manage a lean enterprise today, you must start by making sure your data is lean by eliminating the dirty data that's wasting your time and preventing you from focusing on customers.

Our Team

Evan Liang

Co-founder & Chief Executive Officer

Evan Liang is the CEO and co-founder of LeanData, which he founded after managing data cleanup efforts at his last company and feeling the pain of cleaning Salesforce data by hand. Prior to LeanData, Evan was the GM and VP Products at Caring.com, where he grew a small acquisition into the company’s main business, generating over 50% of the company’s revenue over 2 years. Before Caring.com, Evan worked at Shasta Ventures, a leading venture capital firm focused on end-user-driven businesses. Prior to Shasta, Evan worked in product management at eBay with a focus on buyer experience and, before that, in business development at Microsoft, where he helped launch the Xbox 360. Evan started his career at Battery Ventures, where he specialized in software and e-commerce investments.

Evan holds an MBA from Kellogg and a BS in Industrial Engineering from Stanford University.

Kelvin Cheung

Co-founder & CTO

Kelvin is the CTO and co-founder of LeanData, where he manages the design and architecture of LeanData's application and technology stack. Prior to founding LeanData, Kelvin was the CEO and founder of Wertago, a mobile-app development company that won Google's Android Developer Challenge. Prior to Wertago, he held positions as a software-development manager at both Ooma and Syndeo Corporation. Kelvin started his career as a developer in the Server Technology group at Oracle.

Kelvin holds an MS in Computer Science from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology and BS degrees in both Mathematics and Computer Science also from MIT.

Dale Chen

VP of Engineering & Co-founder

Dale Chen is the VP of Engineering at LeanData, where he leads the development process. Prior to LeanData, Dale was an engineering manager at SAP where he managed and scaled the core engineering team of SAP's social software product. Dale arrived at SAP via the acquisition of CubeTree, an enterprise collaboration suite, which was acquired by SuccessFactors and subsequently bought by SAP. Prior to SAP, Dale held positions in Engineering at Citizen Sports Inc, PeopleSoft, and Homestead Technologies.

Dale holds a BS in Computer Science from Stanford University.

Vighnesh Rege

Founding Engineer

Vighnesh is a Founding Engineer at LeanData, where he helps build services that keep enterprise data clean. Prior to joining LeanData, Vighnesh worked on the TPOT project at Stanford and was a summer intern with the Memcached team at Facebook.
