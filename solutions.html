Why Clean Data Matters

Your CRM system contains mission-critical data. Yet the quality of that data is under constant attack from list uploads, web forms and well-meaning employees who introduce duplicate records, inconsistent formatting and other discrepancies.

Do you trust the quality of your data in Salesforce?

If not, you're probably experiencing the pain of poor CRM data quality: Spending time researching companies that have already been disqualified, Sending redundant emails to existing leads, Duplicate sales calls, Resolving conflict between salespersons over incorrect lead assignments. These don't just waste time and money - they also annoy prospects and build resentment between sales and marketing.

The Solution

Data Quality as a Service. LeanDataTM kicks bad data out and then keeps it out.
