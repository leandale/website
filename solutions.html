The Solution

LeanData combines the power of computer algorithms with the intelligence of humans to efficiently and affordably detect matches, merge records, and enhance data, all within your existing Salesforce application.

Our revolutionary technology delivers 10X improvement in turnaround time while ensuring that you or your staff no longer have to do the tedious, manual data reconciliation work.

Data Quality as a Service. LeanDataTM kicks bad data out and then keeps it out.

A clean database means...

  • Enriched, non-duplicated records that go to the right salesperson the first time.
  • Happy, efficient salespeople who can concentrate on selling.
  • Engaged customers who don't get spammed by duplicate campaign efforts.
