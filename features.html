Features

    End-to-End Process Management

LeanData takes your list, maps it, identifies duplicates and merges the data according to business rules you define. Our ContinuousClean solution extends that to include all accounts and contacts in your CRM.

    Easy Setup

LeanData makes data management simple - starting with the setup. Just download the easy-to-use AppExchange app, which adds a custom tab in your Salesforce instance and enables automated updates with marketing automation systems like Marketo and Eloqua.

    Uploads Made Simple

Once the app is installed, simply upload a CSV file. We will automatically format your data file, map all the fields against your Salesforce instance, and send a test lead to make sure everything is correct. With a few clicks you will be up and running, enjoying the competitive edge of clean data.

    Fuzzy Matching Made Simple

Salesforce knows that "john.doe@company.com" and "john.doe@company.com" are the same person. LeanData's fuzzy algorithms can deduce that "John Doe (jdoe@gmail.com)" and "John Doe (john.doe@yahoo.com)" are likely the same person. But we take it a step further, by leveraging LeanData's distributed on-demand data analysts to verify that they are indeed the same person. By looking across all the available data, including company name, phone, and address fields, LeanData gives you a truly informed judgment and the highest match rates in the industry.

    Flexible Business Rules

LeanData works with you to customize your business rules to resolve all your verified matches. Once we have verified that John Doe at jdoe@gmail.com is the same as John Doe at john.doe@yahoo.com, we'll let you specify how you want us to resolve it. Opt to add a campaign ID to the existing lead, append the email in a second field or any other process. You specify a rule one time and we can apply it to all leads of that category for this data upload or all future uploads. We are here to make sure the data works for you.

    Comprehesive Audit Trail

We show you how the process works, every step of the way. Check up on analysts' matching and mapping, verify any changes you request, and customize business rules at any time.

    Easy Undo

As we're sure you've noticed, Salesforce doesn't have an "undo" feature. Merged the wrong fields? Oops - guess you'll have to delete the whole record and start over. At LeanData, we don't think that's very efficient. The undo feature means you can unmerge records or revise other changes without losing data.

    Real-time Reporting

Our detailed reporting provides status on updated records, status of list imports and ongoing CRM maintenance.

    Complete Data Security

Our algorithms run within your secure Salesforce instance and our analysts review only the data flagged by our algorithms as having potential issues. Data is then anonymized and parsed to different data analysts, who see only the fields necessary to perform their specific task, thus keeping your data secure.
