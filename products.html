Products

LeadStreamTM

Don't want to deal with cleaning up a list before import into Salesforce or your marketing automation system? No problem...

LeadStream offers end-to-end management of inbound leads from webforms and list uploads, including:

  • Mapping
  • Formatting and normalization (e.g., country and state codes)
  • De-duplication
  • Merging and appending
  • 15-minute turnaround on web leads
  • < 48-Hour turnaround on batch lists
  • Optional:
    • Data enrichment (e.g., adding company employees, revenue, website, etc.)
    • Data filtering (e.g., removing or classifying leads from sales regions or industry types not of interest)

CRMCleanTM

CRMClean is a one-time database scrub that includes:

  • Matching, merging and de-duplication of your Salesforce instance by lead, contact and account
  • Enriching (against existing data)
  • 2-Week turnaround
We layer the intelligence of our trained analysts atop smart algorithms to clean your CRM faster and at lower expense than using in-house resources or any 3rd-party solution.

ContinuousCleanTM

Don’t want to ever have to worry about data quality again? No problem...

  • 24-7 monitoring of leads, contacts, and accounts
  • Continuous de-duplication, merging, and appending
  • Continuous management of all lead channels
  • Less than a five-minute turnaround for priority new leads
ContinuousClean holds your data fresh. Marketing automation systems like Marketo and Eloqua run more smoothly and you eliminate redundant outreach and expenditures. Let us keep your data clean and up to date at all times, so that your team keeps selling.
