LeanDataTM Customers Include:

Marketing and Demand Gen Specialists, typically under tight budget constraints, who run the acquisition, retention, and upsell programs necessary to achieve revenue targets.

LeanData marketing customers understand that clean data increases lead conversions and improves prospect segmentation for better customer lifecycle management, thereby increasing marketing ROI and trackability.

    Marketing and Sales Operations

Marketing and Sales Operations Experts who must make sense of CRM and marketing automation data in order to understand what's truly happening within sales and to better align their organizations.

    Sales Managers

Sales Managers who want to eliminate internal strife associated with improper lead assignments and stamp out the waste associated with dirty data. With LeanData, they focus their teams on selling, rather than sorting through questionable or duplicate leads.

    Executives

Executives who understand that ROI tracking of marketing and sales campaigns can't succeed with misleading data.

Our Clients

Our clients are typically mid-sized business-to-business enterprises and rapid growth companies who utilize marketing automation systems such as Eloqua and Marketo alongside Salesforce CRM.

They typically import a significant number of new data elements (such as leads, contacts, and accounts) and have a variety of users who make ongoing changes in systems that inadvertently degrade data quality.
