LeanDataTM Advantages:

   
Highest Quality

LeanData uses a combination of computer algorithms and trained data analysts to resolve fuzzy matches better than any computer-only system and faster than any analyst-only system.

   
Lowest Total Cost of Ownership

The simple setup, administration, and native integration with Salesforce gets you up and running cheaper and faster than other data de-duping products or master data management (MDM) systems.

   
Fast

LeanData has a large, flexible staff of analysts, meaning that it's easy to scale operations at a moment's notice if there's a big spike of lead traffic.

   
Safe and Secure

It's easy to specify the merge rules used, see a secure audit trail of changes made, and instantly undo any changes.
