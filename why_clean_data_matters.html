Why Clean Data Matters

Your CRM system contains mission-critical data. Yet the quality of that data is under constant attack from list uploads, web forms and well-meaning employees who introduce duplicate records, inconsistent formatting and other discrepancies.

Those changes drain productivity and negatively impact revenue.

Do you trust the quality of your data in Salesforce? Would you bet your bonus on it?

If not, you're probably experiencing the pain of poor CRM data quality:

  • Spending time researching companies that have already been disqualified
  • Sending redundant emails to existing leads
  • Duplicate sales calls
  • Resolving conflict between salespersons over incorrect lead assignments.
These don't just waste time and money - they also annoy prospects and build resentment between sales and marketing.
