Benefits

    Poor Data Hygiene

Everyone knows bad data is a problem, but do you realize just how much in revenue dirty data is costing you?

    The Cost of Bad Data

According to SiriusDecisions, up to 25% of B2B marketing leads contain critical errors. Almost 85% of the companies recently surveyed by DemandGen say they are operating CRM and sales databases that house between 10% and 40% bad records.

    Cleaning Up Bad Data Makes a Huge Difference

Campaign results from cleaned up databases show up to a 25% increase in conversion of marketing qualified leads, a 12.5% increase in sales accepted leads, and a 65% increase in revenue.

Other Benefits of Clean Data are Also Clear

   
For Marketing

  • Reduced campaign redundancy and costs
  • Greater ROI tracking
  • More trust with sales

   
For Sales

  • Improved lead scoring and routing
  • Decreased frustration with bad leads
  • Increased sales productivity
